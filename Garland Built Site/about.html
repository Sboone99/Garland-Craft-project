Garland-Craft is a small handbuilt Furniture company based out of Southern Indiana. It is the sister company to Garland-Built contracting and is under the same ownership of Nicholas Garland. Everything we make is made to last and look great while doing so. Don't just have a table, have a table you can be proud of, one made with the highest quality materials and built locally to fit your home and lifestyle.
